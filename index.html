About Rohit Kaushik

Hi there! I am a Technical Designer currently working at Ripple Effect Studios on Battlefield. I graduated from Rochester Institute of Technology, having recieved a Bachelors of Science in Game Design & Development and a minor in Creative Writing. I am very passionate about game design. I strive to create fun and lasting experiences for players and hope to inspire people through my games. I enjoy programming, and have experience in developing with many industry standard languages, such as C/C++, C#, Python, etc. I have also worked with AAA game engines and have experience in Frostbite, Unreal, and Unity. While I am currently not looking for employment opportunities, please feel free to reach out!
